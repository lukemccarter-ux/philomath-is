About

The short version

I manage maintenance operations for industrial processing and manufacturing. Asset counts in the thousands across multiple divisions and sites. My professional life is CMMS management, predictive maintenance, environmental compliance, and building data pipelines to help make the invisible parts of operations visible.

The creative

Lifelong violinist and drummer. Listening habits run from Mozart to Meshuggah, Getz to Gojira, Brahms to Beastie Boys. Stipple artist and illustrator. VW tuning enthusiast. Custom watchmaker: sourcing movements, cases, dials, hands, and bracelets to design and assemble full builds from the ground up. Trained audio engineer (Fanshawe College, Music Industry Arts) with a project studio. The thread is figuring out how to do stuff I find interesting without relying on prefab options. Just find some tools and start tinkering.

The technical

Wannabe tech nerd since my first DOS command on a Commodore 64. Built Geocities sites before Google existed. Decades of hands-on experience across personal, business, and creative software categories. Now building AI-assisted tools, industrial IoT pipelines, and workflow automation. Still just as curious about what the next command does. Active member of the Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI).

Things I'm building

Open Source

Priority Pulse

A personal AI assistant that watches how you actually spend your time and tells you what you're avoiding. Built for ADHD brains in high-interrupt jobs where the day disappears before you've touched the important stuff.

Python · Claude API (Haiku for classification, Sonnet for analysis) · OAuth 2.0 multi-source ingestion (Gmail, Google Calendar/Tasks, Outlook ICS) · Power Automate pipeline · Obsidian vault output · Local-first, no cloud, no telemetry
In Progress

Part Lokker

Drop a photo of a quote, invoice, or packing slip and get a clean, ready-to-import file for your CMMS. No manual data entry, no reformatting spreadsheets. Handles PDFs, scanned documents, and photos from the shop floor.

AI document extraction (OCR + LLM) · .pdf, .doc, .jpeg, .png input · Auto-formatted .csv to CMMS import spec · Field mapping for vendor-specific formats · Batch processing
In Progress

Diagnostic Assistant

An AI coach that walks technicians through diagnosis and repair of niche industrial systems. Uses manufacturer documentation first, asks for specific photos or videos mid-conversation, and guides rather than guesses.

RAG over manufacturer service manuals · Multi-turn conversational UI · In-context image/video analysis · Guided decision trees with conditional branching · Per-equipment knowledge base
In Progress

Scrap Metal ID

A mobile app that helps yard workers identify metal grades on the spot. The model improves continuously because the people using it are the ones training it. Designed to fit into existing workflows without adding steps.

Custom ML image classification · On-device inference · Continuous learning: expert corrections feed training data · Workflow-integrated capture/classify/confirm UX · Ferrous + non-ferrous multi-grade taxonomy
Parked

VRespite

VR applications with two focus areas: rapid-access sensory environments for students experiencing autism-related sensory overloads at school, and custom-built immersive experiences for long-term and palliative care patients. Co-founded.

360° video production · Purpose-built VR environments · Clinical + educational deployment · Sensory modulation research-informed
